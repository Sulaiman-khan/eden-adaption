Who we Are

Eden Adaption Limited

As based in the heart of Birmingham, Eden Adaptions specialises in high quality building work, including new builds, renovations, bathroom refurbishments and disability adaptations including access.

Eden Adaptions Ltd provides a bespoke service which meets all our client’s needs. Eden Adaptions Ltd is a well-known company with a long history of experience, quality, efficiency and attention to detail. Eden Adaptions Ltd has gathered a strong portfolio from its clients in the West Midlands and has created recognition through our impressive reputation with local councils

If you want to improve your house or apartment by renovating, converting your attic, extensions or you are building a new house. We can do this for you, by providing high quality design and inspiration with a wide variety of construction materials. All our staff are fully trained, experienced and well equipped in helping our customers receive the best professional and personal service available. Our aim is to ensure that you receive a friendly and reliable service whilst maintaining a high level of workmanship at competitive prices
